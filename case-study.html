Supply Chain Analytics

Optimizing Inventory Management with Power BI & SQL

How I reduced inventory waste by 15% and saved $50k annually through data-driven forecasting.

Role Lead Data Analyst
Timeline 3 Months
Tools Power BI, SQL, Python
Dashboard Screenshot / Visual

The Challenge

The client, a mid-sized manufacturing firm, was facing significant profit leakage due to poor inventory management. They were relying on manual Excel sheets to track stock levels across 3 warehouses, leading to:

  • Overstocking: Capital tied up in slow-moving goods.
  • Stockouts: Lost sales opportunities during peak demand.
  • Data Latency: Reports were always 1-2 weeks old.

The Data-Driven Solution

My approach focused on automating the data pipeline and creating a self-service intelligence layer.

01. Data Engineering

Built an automated ETL pipeline using Python to extract data from the ERP system and load it into a SQL warehouse.

02. Data Modeling

Designed a Star Schema in Power BI to optimize query performance and enable complex DAX calculations for "Days Sales of Inventory" (DSI).

03. Visualization

Developed an interactive dashboard with alerts for low stock and AI-driven demand forecasting.

The Business Impact

15% Reduction in Waste
$50k Annual Savings
30 hrs Manual Work Saved/Month
